"Cloud Computing vs Edge Computing: What's Powering the Smart World?"**
## Skit Title

**"Cloud vs Edge: The Battle for the Smart World"**

**Duration:** 8–10 minutes

### Characters (10 members)

1. Narrator
    
2. Cloud Computing
    
3. Edge Computing
    
4. IoT Smart Camera
    
5. Autonomous Car
    
6. Hospital Doctor
    
7. Student
    
8. Business Owner
    
9. Hacker
    
10. Judge (Technology Expert)
    

---

## Scene 1 – Introduction (Narrator)

**Narrator:**

> Welcome everyone! Today we'll witness a debate between two powerful technologies—Cloud Computing and Edge Computing. Who is better? Let's find out through real-life situations.

_(Cloud enters from one side wearing a cloud cutout or blue costume. Edge enters from the other wearing a lightning or "EDGE" badge.)_

---

## Scene 2 – The Debate

**Cloud:**

> Hello everyone! I store massive amounts of data and provide services anywhere through the internet. Companies love me because I'm scalable and cost-effective.

**Edge:**

> That's true, but I'm faster! I process data near the device itself, reducing delay. When every second matters, I'm the hero.

---

## Scene 3 – Smart Camera

_(IoT Camera enters.)_

**Camera:**

> I record thousands of videos every day. Should I send everything to Cloud?

**Cloud:**

> Yes! I have huge storage and can analyze everything later.

**Edge:**

> Wait! I can detect suspicious movement immediately and alert security without waiting for internet communication.

**Narrator:**

> Winner: **Edge Computing** because low latency is essential.

---

## Scene 4 – Autonomous Car

_(Car enters holding a steering wheel.)_

**Car:**

> A child suddenly crosses the road! Should I wait for Cloud to respond?

**Cloud:**

> Internet communication may take some time...

**Edge:**

> I'll process the sensor data instantly and apply the brakes.

_(Car pretends to stop just in time.)_

**Narrator:**

> Winner: **Edge Computing** because milliseconds save lives.

---

## Scene 5 – Student

**Student:**

> I need Google Drive, Gmail, and online classes.

**Cloud:**

> That's my specialty! I store your files safely and let you access them anywhere.

**Edge:**

> I help too by reducing delay, but your storage belongs mostly to Cloud.

**Narrator:**

> Winner: **Cloud Computing**

---

## Scene 6 – Business Owner

**Business Owner:**

> My company grows every month. I need more storage.

**Cloud:**

> No problem! Increase your storage instantly without buying servers.

**Business Owner:**

> Amazing! That saves money.

---

## Scene 7 – Hospital Emergency

**Doctor:**

> My patient wears a smart heart monitor.

**Edge:**

> I'll detect abnormal heartbeat immediately and alert the doctor.

**Cloud:**

> I'll store the patient's history and generate long-term health reports.

**Doctor:**

> I need both!

---

## Scene 8 – Hacker

_(Hacker enters wearing sunglasses and black clothes.)_

**Hacker:**

> I'll attack your data!

**Cloud:**

> I provide encryption, backups, and advanced security.

**Edge:**

> Since I process data locally, less sensitive data travels across the network.

**Narrator:**

> Both technologies improve security in different ways.

---

## Scene 9 – Judge's Decision

**Judge:**

> After hearing both sides, I declare...

> Edge Computing is best when:
> 
> - Fast response is needed
>     
> - IoT devices
>     
> - Smart cities
>     
> - Self-driving cars
>     
> - AR/VR
>     

> Cloud Computing is best when:
> 
> - Large storage is required
>     
> - Data analytics
>     
> - Web applications
>     
> - Email
>     
> - File sharing
>     

> Together they create the smart world!

---

## Final Message (All Together)

**Everyone:**

> Cloud and Edge are not competitors—they are teammates.

> **Edge processes data quickly.**  
> **Cloud stores and analyzes data globally.**

> **Together, they power the smart world!**

---

## Simple Props

- Cotton cloud cutout for Cloud.
    
- "EDGE" badge or lightning symbol.
    
- Cardboard steering wheel for the car.
    
- Toy CCTV camera or phone for the smart camera.
    
- Stethoscope for the doctor.
    
- Laptop or books for the student.
    
- Black hoodie and sunglasses for the hacker.
    
- Gavel or clipboard for the judge.
    

## Audience Engagement

At the end, ask:

- "If your car must stop in one second, would you choose Cloud or Edge?"
    
- "If you need 1 TB of online storage, which would you choose?"
    

Then reveal the answers:

- **Car → Edge Computing**
    
- **Online Storage → Cloud Computing**
