
|**Model**|**Best For...**|**Requirements**|**Risk Level**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Waterfall**|Simple, short projects|**Fixed** & crystal clear|Low (if well-defined)|
|**Iterative**|Complex, evolving systems|High-level (refined later)|Medium|
|**Incremental**|Fast "time-to-market"|Modular / Prioritized|Low to Medium|
|**Prototype**|High UI/UX focus|**Unclear** or "vague"|Medium|
|**RAD**|Rapid business apps|Well-known / Tool-driven|Low (for experienced teams)|
|**Spiral**|Massive, mission-critical|Complex / Experimental|**High** (The risk-killer)|
