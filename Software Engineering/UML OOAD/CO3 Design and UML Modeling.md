These questions evaluate your ability to create structural and behavioral models and understand design principles.

- **Design Principles:**
    - Discuss the statement: "A design solution is simple and understandable if it is **modular**" and explain how to achieve this modularity.
    - Justify whether an **increase in cohesion** automatically leads to a **decrease in coupling**.
- **Structural Modeling:**
    - Design a simple **Class diagram** for ATM Banking software.
- **Behavioral Modeling:**
    - **State Machine Diagrams:** Draw a **state chart** for a device with three radio buttons (b1, b2, b3) that are initially off, where pressing one turns it on and others off.
    - **Sequence Diagrams:** Design a sequence diagram for the **Coffee Machine Problem**.
    - **Activity Diagrams:**
        - Narrate the process of **withdrawing money from an ATM**.
        - Explain steps for **online shopping** or **online food ordering** using a mobile app.
    - **Collaboration Diagrams:** Often asked as an alternative (OR) to sequence diagrams, specifically for the **ATM cash withdrawal** scenario.
