### 1. Placement Season Update

- The placement scenario has changed this year.
- Earlier, the Placement Office scheduled companies based on profile and package.
- Now, **companies decide when they want to visit**, and the college has to accommodate their schedules.
- So, don't expect companies to visit in a particular order.

### 2. Don't Skip Early Opportunities

Many students wonder:

> "What if a better company comes later?"

The Placement Office clarified that:

- They **cannot guarantee** a higher-package company will visit later.
- If a company is ready to recruit now, PSG Tech will allow the drive.
- Skipping an early opportunity could mean missing out altogether.

### 3. Single Offer Policy

- Once you accept an offer, you **cannot continue attending interviews** simply because another company offers a higher package.
- A detailed circular regarding the package cutoff (around ₹4 LPA was mentioned) will be shared soon.

### 4. TCS Update

- **Online Test:** **19 July**
- Interviews will follow.
- TCS is offering **multiple job profiles/packages** this year.
- The college opted for the **July drive instead of waiting until August** to avoid missing recruitment opportunities.

### 5. Internship PPO Timeline

Students currently doing internships:

- Companies have been requested to announce **Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs)** before **15 July**.
- Full-time placement drives are expected to begin after that.

### 6. Placement Portal Deadline

**Deadline: 10 July**

Please verify:

- 10th marks
- 12th marks
- PAN details
- Aadhaar details
- Personal information
- Academic details

After **10 July**, no edits will be allowed as the data will be shared with companies.

> **Note:** CGPA will be updated by the Placement Office.

### 7. Students Planning Higher Studies

If you're **not planning to sit for placements**, please **opt out**.

Reason:

- Blocking an offer and later rejecting it affects PSG Tech's relationship with recruiters.

### 8. Communication with HR

Students should **not contact company HRs directly** regarding placements.

All communication should follow:

**Student → PR → Placement Office → HR**

### 9. Placement Orientation

Department-wise orientation sessions (30–45 minutes) will be conducted covering:

- Placement process
- Resume preparation
- Placement rules
- Company registration
- Placement portal

### 10. Resume

Students can upload **company-specific resumes** on the placement portal.

Please keep your resume updated.

### 11. Eligibility & Preparation

The Placement Office appreciated students who:

- Completed internships
- Participated in hackathons
- Built industry-relevant skills
- Cleared arrears

They also emphasized:

> **CGPA alone does not guarantee placement.**  
> Knowledge, projects, internships, communication skills, and seriousness matter more.

### 12. Message from the Dean

The Dean encouraged everyone to:

- Focus on hard work rather than luck.
- Prepare consistently for placements.
- Clear arrears as early as possible.
- Maintain discipline and professionalism.
- Trust that the Placement Office will continue bringing as many opportunities as possible.

## Immediate Action Items

- Verify your placement portal details before **10 July**.
- Ensure PAN, Aadhaar, marks, and personal details are correct.
- Prepare for the **TCS Online Test (19 July)**.
- Keep your resume updated.
- Decide whether you're opting for placements or higher studies.
- Do not contact company HRs directly.
- Route all placement-related queries through the MCA PRs.

Good luck to everyone!
